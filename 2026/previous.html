{{ site.workshop_short_name }} @ IJCKG 2025

The first edition of {{ site.workshop_short_name }} was held on October 15-17, 2025, at Heraklion, Greece, co-located with IJCKG 2025.

The workshop aimed to bring together researchers from databases, knowledge management, and quantum computing, and provided a forum for presenting early results, discussing open challenges, and identifying promising directions for hybrid classical-quantum data-centric systems.

The workshop attracted submissions on a broad range of topics, including quantum query processing, quantum-enhanced retrieval, quantum machine learning for knowledge extraction, and quantum knowledge representation. The program included paper presentations and community discussions that helped shape a research agenda for quantum-aware data systems.
