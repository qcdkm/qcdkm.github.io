Keynote Speakers

{{ site.title }} will feature two invited keynote talks by leading researchers and practitioners at the intersection of quantum computing and data/knowledge management. Speakers will be selected for their contributions to quantum computing and/or data and knowledge management, their ability to address both theoretical and systems-level aspects, and the relevance of their work to real-world data-centric and hybrid classical-quantum applications.
